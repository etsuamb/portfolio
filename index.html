About Me

Hello, I am Etsub Girma. I am currently a fourth-year Software Engineering student at Addis Ababa Science and Technology University. I am passionate about advancing my skills in Flutter development, driven by a strong interest in building innovative and functional applications.

I have primarily focused on Flutter development and have gained hands-on experience in creating various app projects. In addition to mobile apps, I have contributed to web development projects, which has broadened my understanding of software development practices. My goal is to bring my ideas to life by creating useful, impactful applications, and I am also eager to expand my knowledge of related technologies, such as Python.
